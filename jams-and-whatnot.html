High Sails

2-6 player naval couch combat 

Local multiplayer competitive sailing/shooting game made for Fitchburg State University's Fall 2022 game jam in Godot 3.5. I programmed everything in GDScript while my friend Ryan did the art.

Programming highlights:

  • Movement - players move automatically, can only steer. Getting caught in a current causes the player to speed up, ramming into someone while going fast enough causes an instant kill
  • Safe respawn system - randomly picks a space to spawn, checks if any players are in it, if so spawn somewhere else, repeat until a spot is safe
   

Bears vs Humans

2 player assymetric hide and seek

Another local multiplayer game, this time an assymetric hunting game where one player plays as one of 3-5 bears while another plays as one of 20 humans. Both try to blend in, acting like the other AI actors, while the Bear tries to attack the real human. Made in Unity for my Game Design Workshop class in 2020.
